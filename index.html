About Ghana Shippers' Authority

Leading Ghana's maritime industry with integrity, innovation, and excellence

Our Mission

To protect the interests of shippers, promote efficient and competitive shipping services, and facilitate Ghana's international trade through effective regulation and advocacy.

Our Vision

To be the premier maritime regulatory authority in West Africa, ensuring efficient, competitive, and sustainable shipping services for Ghana's economic growth.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Transparency, and Commitment to serving Ghana's shipping community with the highest professional standards.

Our Services

Comprehensive maritime and shipping services for Ghana's trade community

Trade Facilitation

Streamlining import and export processes, reducing bureaucratic bottlenecks, and enhancing trade efficiency through our ports.

  • Cargo clearance assistance
  • Documentation support
  • Process optimization

Regulatory Oversight

Ensuring compliance with maritime regulations, monitoring shipping rates, and maintaining fair competition in the industry.

  • Rate regulation
  • Compliance monitoring
  • Industry standards

Dispute Resolution

Mediating conflicts between shippers and service providers, ensuring fair resolution of maritime trade disputes.

  • Mediation services
  • Arbitration support
  • Legal guidance

Market Intelligence

  • Market analysis
  • Trend reports
  • Statistical data

Training & Capacity Building

Developing human resources in the maritime sector through specialized training programs and workshops.

  • Certification programs
  • Skills development
